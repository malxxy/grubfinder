About GrubFinder

GrubFinder was founded by four classmates who were always on the hunt for their favorite type of foods.

We created this food finder for those who were also on that same mission; whether you are looking for a specific type of:

    -Cuisine
    -Dietary Preference
    -Pricing

GrubFinder provides the means of searching for these eateries in a location near you with a few simple clicks!

Simply use your location or feel free to type in an address to display on the map the variety of different options!
